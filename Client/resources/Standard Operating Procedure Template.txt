PROCESS MEASUREMENT AND AUTHORIZATION
(Track how each task is validated, documented, and approved.)

Example table layout:

\| Task | Inner Measure of Success | Outer Measure of Success | Reporting | Authorization |

Populate each row as follows:
• **Task** – name or short description of the activity.
• **Inner Measure of Success** – how the implementer confirms the task was completed correctly (technical proof, checklist, log entry).
• **Outer Measure of Success** – how the client, end user, or stakeholder experiences success (perceived value, reduced downtime, satisfaction).
• **Reporting** – where and how completion is documented (ticketing system, inventory update, final report).
• **Authorization** – roles, approvals, or system permissions required to perform the task.

GENERAL NOTES
